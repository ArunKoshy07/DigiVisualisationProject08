Invisible Dangers: An Analysis of Pollution in Cities

A vital element of a thriving city is a healthy environment. Many elements can impact its health, and are often invisible and overlooked. The main source of damage is pollution. These pollutants come in many forms but we will focus on the 4 main types: air pollution, light pollution, waste pollution and noise pollution. The impact of these pollutants are devastating to city and its population. These visulisations will aim to demonstrate the impact of pollution on a city in its different forms.

Noise Pollution

Noise or Unwanted sound can effect our health, wellbeing, productivity and natural enviroment. Studies have shown that noise is a cause for sleep disturbance, cardiovascular and psychophysiological issues, affect performance, provoke annoyance and affects the social behaviour in humans. As per Department for Environment,Food & Rural Affairs UK, the annual social cost of urban road noise in England is £7 to 10 billion, placing it similar to road accidents(£9billion) and significantly greater than climate change(£1-4 billion).
